Walk-in Services

Walk-into any of our centres to experience our care first-hand. Call us to consult with a doctor, schedule a health check, order a set of blood tests or X-rays with a prescription. You can even call us for home delivery of medicines or ask for a doctor to come home for a consultation.
Corporate Services

Our integrated care programs go beyond just a health check. We also work with your colleagues to improve organisational health throughout the year. This may mean fun team building wellness events, doctor at work, physio boot camps or nutrition consults.
Occupational Health Services

We offer robust occupational healthcare solutions capable of catering to the needs of large organizations in accordance with the Factories Act. This includes - Set-up & Management of the Occupational Health Centre, Periodic Health Evaluations, Health & Wellness Workshops, 24x7 Tele-Health & 24x7 On-Site Medical Emergency Care.
Our Vision
It is our endeavour to have a chain of high quality Health Centres with all the services required to bridge the huge gap that exists at present in people’s health care needs. Each Healthspring Centre in the location brings all the health care services under one roof- for individual, family, community, corporate houses and Industrial complexes.
24x7 Medical Care
The aim is to set up new standards of healthcare delivery at the pre-hospital space, based on our founding principles of excellence in clinical care,being sensitive to the patient’s needs,time, convenience and dependability and to provide service 24 hours across 365 days !
