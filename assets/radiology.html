Radiology

Our approach to diagnostic testing – everything you need, nothing you don’t. So all routine radiology and other equipment is available under the same roof, in the same clinic. We will not send you chasing to another centre for routine tests after you have met the doctor!

Each of our centres houses high intensity X-ray, Treadmill, Electrocardiogram and Pulmonary Function Test facilities. Several of our centres also house UltraSound equipment.

All these tests are also available on Sundays, by appointment. Our X-rays are given to you on a CD, so you can keep them forever.

To schedule a test, call your nearest centre now . Remember to carry your prescription and call and ask for specific test instructions

We are able to coordinate priority CT and MRI scans when required with our

neighbourhood partner centres. So you don’t have to wait for an appointment!
